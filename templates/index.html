Frequently Asked Questions

How does the ranking work?
The system uses TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency) and cosine similarity to rank candidates. This ensures that the most relevant resumes match your search criteria accurately.
Which languages are supported?
Currently, we support English, Kazakh, and Russian resumes. Our system can accurately analyze and rank resumes in all these languages.
How accurate is the matching?
Our system achieves high accuracy through advanced natural language processing techniques. The TF-IDF algorithm ensures that the most relevant candidates are ranked higher based on your specific requirements.
